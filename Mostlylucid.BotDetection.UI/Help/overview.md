# Dashboard Overview

The Overview tab is your starting point -- a real-time snapshot of everything StyloBot has observed about traffic to your site. It pulls together detection results, geographic data, and activity trends into a single screen so you can take the pulse of your site at a glance.

## What you'll see

- **Summary cards** across the top -- total requests, bot percentage, human percentage, and unique bot signatures detected.
- **Traffic chart** -- a time series showing request volume broken into bots, humans, and threats.
- **Recent activity feed** -- a live list of the most recent requests with their classification.
- **Top bots sidebar** -- the most active bot signatures seen in the current window.
- **Active endpoints** -- which paths on your site are receiving the most traffic right now.
- **Global threat map** -- a world map with heat markers showing where traffic is coming from.

## Where to look first

If something feels off -- a spike in load, unusual error rates -- open the Overview tab and scan the bot percentage card and the traffic chart. A sharp rise in bot traffic alongside a drop in human traffic is the clearest early warning sign.

**Tip:** The overview refreshes automatically. If you leave it open in a browser tab it will stay current without manual reloads.
